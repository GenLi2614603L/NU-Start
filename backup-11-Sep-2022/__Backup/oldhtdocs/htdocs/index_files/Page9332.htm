There are a number of services that Nu Start offers young people.  These services range from diversionary activities to employment and training opportunities that equips the young person with new skills, provides experiences and knowledge that will benefit them throughout their lives.


We offer a range of Courses, Workshops, Training, Practical skills, Counselling and Personal Development Solutions to motivate and instil a work ethic in young people, thus creating a more confident and positive mind set.

We provide a combination of professional facilitators, trainers & actors within our training programmes and devise tailored written training modules to suit a required theme.

Nu Start Community Enterprise provides the necessary skills to support the young people and the community to be actively involved in economic activity and ultimately economic independence and as an avenue for practical input into the financial and social well being of their community. 

 

Nu Start Community Enterprise supports people to work together in their communities to develop and undertake social and commercial activities that are often missing within their community.
