Nu Start Community Enterprise recognises that it is important to deliver services for women, both young and mature that can meet their needs and offer support on issues such as domestic violence, sexually exploitation, substance misuse and other related factors.

 

We do this by engaging women into our services that are shaped and designed so that women are at the forefront of our delivery.

All of our courses are gender specific (no men allowed) which gives women the freedom and scope to discuss and share their experiences without the fear of persecution and guilt, thus creating a safe environment for them to express their feelings, discuss their situation and receive support from their peers and professionals, which in turn meets their needs.

 

All of our services are delivered holistically.

 

We do this by delivering a number of diversionary activities, alternative therapies and more orthodox forms of treatment, i.e. Psychotherapy and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy.

 

The content of the Women’s only service includes:

 

· Women in Business

· Modelling Workshop

· Dance Classes

· 1-2-1 counselling

· Group therapy

· Acupuncture

· Reiki

· Hair and Make-up

· Dress-making
