Delroy has been working in the social care field for over 15 years and is currently a Director of Nu Start Community Enterprise. He has worked across all sectors, including young people, substance misuse, criminal justice, housing, mental health and BME provisions.  He has worked across all levels from practitioner, management and commissioner for a number of organisations. 

Elly is a Chartered Clinical Psychologist who works part-time for the NSPCC, where she assesses and treats young people with sexually abusive behaviour, and provides psychological consultation to NSPCC teams regarding childhood sexual abuse. She is also involved in the NSPCC’s emerging work on sexual bullying in schools. Elly works therapeutically with adults with substance misuse problems, with a focus on the resolution of trauma. Her work also includes writing and research, she has co-developed a therapy manual, and is contributing to books regarding offending, substance misuse and trauma. Fields in which Elly has previously worked include children and adolescent mental health, youth offending (in HMP/YOI Feltham) and adult trauma (predominantly refugees).
